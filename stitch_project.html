Redesign Customer Portal

A comprehensive overhaul of the client-facing dashboard to improve usability, accessibility, and align with the new editorial design system.

Project Brief

The current customer portal has become fragmented over years of iterative updates. This project aims to consolidate the user experience into a single, cohesive interface adhering to our new tactile, editorial design principles.

Key objectives include:

  • Implementing the new fixed-grid layout system.
  • Upgrading all typography to the Manrope/Newsreader/Inter stack.
  • Removing all synthetic gradients and rounded corners in favor of crisp 1px borders.
  • Ensuring full WCAG 2.2 AA compliance across all views.

This document serves as the central source of truth for the project's requirements, timelines, and ongoing discussions.
